The Document IS the Context Window

01

YOUR PROJECT IS THE AI'S MEMORY

The entire file—text, data, diagrams, code—is the living context. The AI understands your whole project, not just a highlighted paragraph.

02

EDITING IS YOUR PRIMARY INTERACTION

You don't prompt. You edit. As the AI streams its draft directly onto the canvas, you refine it in real-time. Your edits continuously update the context and shape the AI's understanding.

03

THE AI LEARNS FROM EVERY EDIT

The system captures the delta between the AI's raw output and your final version. That difference trains the AI to match your style, tone, and judgment. Over time, it becomes YOUR AI.

Not a chatbot in a sidebar. A partner on your canvas.
